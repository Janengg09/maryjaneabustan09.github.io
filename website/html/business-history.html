BUSINESS HISTORY



Crispy Chicken Strips and Sip Haven, established on June 14, 2024, has quickly become a beloved destination for those in search of the ultimate comfort food experience. Our specialty lies in serving golden, crispy chicken strips, perfectly seasoned to offer an irresistible crunch and flavor in every bite. Whether you're in the mood for a satisfying meal or a quick, flavorful snack, our menu has something to please every palate. Each dish is crafted with care to ensure that every customer leaves with a smile, savoring the experience long after the last bite.

In addition to our signature chicken strips, we offer a diverse selection of refreshing beverages to complement your meal. From classic sodas to unique specialty drinks, there's something for everyone to enjoy. At Crispy Chicken Strips and Sip Haven, we pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where every visit feels like a mini getaway. Whether you're dining in, grabbing takeout, or enjoying a solo snack break, we invite you to come and indulge in a taste experience that satisfies your cravings and refreshes your spirit.
